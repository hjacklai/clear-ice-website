CARE GUIDE

Get the most
out of your ice.

  1. 01

    Temper first.

    Rest the ice at room temperature for 2 to 3 minutes before use. Skipping this lets thermal shock crack and cloud your cube the moment liquid hits it.

  2. 02

    Pour on the side.

    Especially for engraved cubes. The logo surface melts fastest. Tilt the glass and pour along the side. Logo stays sharp, drink still chills evenly.

  3. 03

    Tongs, not fingers.

    Fingerprints and hand warmth dull the surface and start it melting. Tongs or gloved hands keep every cube crystal-clear, glass to glass.

How is clear ice different from regular ice?+

Regular freezer ice freezes from all sides at once, trapping air, freezer odours, and dissolved minerals. That is why it is cloudy and melts fast. Our clear ice is directionally frozen over 72 hours, then hand-cut and polished. Zero cloud, no off-flavours, melts up to 3 times slower than standard cubes.
