How the estimate works.

We assume a typical guest has roughly one drink per hour. Cocktails and whisky neat use one large piece of ice per drink (cube or sphere). Highballs use one cylinder per drink. We then round up to the nearest box of 25 (our minimum order unit) and add a small safety buffer.
