Clear ice column.
Square and tall.

A square-edged clear ice column, available in 4 cm by 4 cm by 10 cm or 3.5 cm by 3.5 cm by 11 cm. Cousin to the cylinder, but with sharp geometric edges that look intentional in tall glassware. Bars use it for theatrical presentation. Cafes use it where the cylinder will not fit a narrower glass. RM 3.00 per piece.

Specs.

  • Dimensions. 4 cm x 4 cm x 10 cm, or 3.5 cm x 3.5 cm x 11 cm. We pick the size that fits your glass best, or you tell us.
  • Price. RM 3.00 per column. Minimum 25 pieces.
  • Glassware. Tall Collins glass, narrow highball, or stemless tumbler. Especially useful for narrower glassware where a wider cylinder will not fit.
  • Made by. Directional freezing, then square-cut and hand polished.
  • Delivery. Same-day across KL and Selangor for orders confirmed by 2 PM, traffic permitting.

Where it shines.

Presentation cocktails

A square column slides into a clean tall glass and looks like sculpture. Useful when the drink is the wow moment.

Long-pour cocktails

Tom Collins, mojito-style builds, paloma. The column keeps the entire vertical chilled instead of just the bottom inch.

Narrow tall glasses

Where a round cylinder will not seat properly, the square column does, because the corners catch the glass walls.

Iced specialty drinks

Iced matcha, iced chai, layered cold brews. The column does not displace much volume so you keep the drink build intact.

Care tip.

Temper for 2-3 minutes at room temperature before contact with liquid. Drop the column in first, then pour slowly along the side of the glass. Full care guide →
