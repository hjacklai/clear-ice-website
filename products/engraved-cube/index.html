Engraved clear cube.
Your logo. In ice.

A 5 cm crystal-clear cube with your logo, monogram or a guest's name engraved into the face. The ice acts as the canvas. Send us a logo file, we engrave each cube, you serve it at the event. RM 4.00 per cube, 25-piece minimum. Lead time is 2 to 3 days, depending on logo complexity, so please WhatsApp us early.

What you send us.

  • The logo or text. SVG, PNG with transparent background, or a high-resolution PDF works best. Simple line art engraves cleanest.
  • The quantity. Minimum 25 pieces. Mix engraved with plain cubes if you want a partial-engraving event.
  • The date and address. So we can plan the engraving slot and the delivery.
  • Optional. Two-tone engraving (logo plus name, for example) is possible. Tell us what you want.

When it works best.

Hotel signatures

Your hotel's monogram engraved on every welcome cocktail. Subtle, permanent enough for the duration of the drink, premium signal.

Weddings

Couple's initials or wedding date. Photographs beautifully. Guests notice.

Brand launches

Your brand mark on every drink at the launch event. Custom, unrepeatable, photographable.

Corporate gifting

Engraved cubes packed individually as gifts at gala dinners, executive events, anniversaries.

Anniversaries, birthdays

Year, initials, or short message engraved into the cube. Drink-side keepsake without keeping anything physical.

Lead time and limits.

Engraved orders are not same-day. Typical lead time is 2 to 3 days from the moment we have a usable logo file. Very intricate logos or large quantities may take longer; we will quote you when you send the brief. Please WhatsApp the file early so we can plan around the event date.

Care tips for engraved cubes.

The engraved face melts a little faster than the rest of the cube because it has more surface area. Tilt the glass and pour the spirit down the opposite side so the engraving stays sharp for as long as possible. Temper for 2-3 minutes at room temperature before use. Full care guide →
