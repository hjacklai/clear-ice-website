Clear ice sphere.
For whisky.

A 5.5 cm crystal-clear sphere. Of all solid shapes, a sphere has the smallest surface area for its volume, which is why it tends to be the slowest-melting option of the lineup. Whisky drinkers like that. So do anyone serving a spirit they want to chill without watering down. RM 3.50 per sphere, 25-piece minimum.

Specs.

  • Diameter. 5.5 cm.
  • Price. RM 3.50 per sphere. Minimum 25 pieces.
  • Glassware. Pairs best with a rocks glass or a Glencairn-style whisky glass. Fits comfortably in standard tumblers.
  • Made by. Directional freezing, then turned and finished by hand into the sphere shape.
  • Delivery. Same-day across KL and Selangor for orders confirmed by 2 PM, traffic permitting.

Where it shines.

Whisky neat

Single malt, bourbon, rye, Japanese whisky. The sphere chills the pour slowly so the spirit's profile opens up without watering down.

Aged spirits

Aged rum, anejo tequila, cognac. Same principle. Slow chill, slow open-up.

Presentation pours

The sphere is the most photogenic shape. Looks intentional in any glass.

Premium cocktails

Sphere in a Sazerac, Vieux Carre, Brandy Old Fashioned. Works wherever you want a single-piece stir, slow dilution build.

Care tip.

Like all clear ice, the sphere benefits from a 2-3 minute temper at room temperature before contact with liquid. This prevents thermal-shock fractures. Full care guide →
