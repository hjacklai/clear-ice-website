Clear ice cylinder.
For highballs.

A 4 cm by 11 cm crystal-clear cylinder. Sits tall in a highball glass, chills the pour from top to bottom, and looks like a piece of architecture. The shape Japanese bartenders use for the classic whisky highball, and the shape that has become standard for serious gin and tonic and craft cold brew. RM 3.50 per piece.

Specs.

  • Dimensions. 4 cm diameter, 11 cm tall.
  • Price. RM 3.50 per cylinder. Minimum 25 pieces.
  • Glassware. Standard highball, Collins glass, or tall tumbler. The cylinder is designed to sit upright with about a finger of clearance at the top.
  • Made by. Directional freezing, then cut to cylindrical form.
  • Delivery. Same-day across KL and Selangor for orders confirmed by 2 PM, traffic permitting.

Where it shines.

Japanese highball

Whisky and soda, served tall over a single clear cylinder. The cylinder chills the entire pour at once, evenly, and the slow melt keeps the highball crisp.

Gin and tonic

One cylinder, the gin, the tonic over the back of a spoon. Botanical drinks need clean ice. This is it.

Vodka soda, vodka tonic

Same logic. Spirit plus mixer, tall pour, the cylinder keeps it cold without diluting fast.

Cold brew, iced latte, espresso tonic

Speciality coffee in a tall glass. The cylinder lets the bean's flavour stay clean from first sip to last.

Iced tea, kombucha

When you want a tall, clean drink that does not water down halfway.

Care tip.

Temper the cylinder for 2-3 minutes at room temperature before pouring. Drop it into the glass first, then pour slowly down the side to avoid thermal shock. Full care guide →
