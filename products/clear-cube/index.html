Clear ice cube.
Our signature.

The 5 cm or 5.5 cm crystal-clear cube is our signature shape. It is the everyday workhorse of the lineup and the shape most spirit-forward cocktails are built around. One cube to a glass. Slow melt. Clean finish. RM 3.00 each, 25-piece minimum, mix-and-match with other shapes.

Specs.

  • Dimensions. 5 cm x 5 cm x 5 cm, or 5.5 cm x 5.5 cm x 5.5 cm. We pick the size that fits your glass best, or you tell us.

Where it shines.

Old Fashioned, Negroni, Manhattan

The classic single-cube cocktail build. The cube takes up enough volume to chill without over-diluting. Spirit-forward stays spirit-forward.

Whisky on the rocks

One 5 cm cube in a rocks glass. Slow melt suits single malts, bourbons and rye.

Espresso tonic, cold brew

Speciality coffee. The cube does not flatten the bean's flavour profile the way freezer ice does.

Mocktails, iced tea

When you want the drink to be the focus and the ice to be quiet.

Two quick care tips.

Rest the cube at room temperature for 2 to 3 minutes before use. This tempering step prevents thermal-shock cracks the moment liquid hits it. And pour slowly down the side of the glass, not directly on the cube. Full care guide →
